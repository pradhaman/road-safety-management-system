🚨 What to do in a road emergency

  1. Stay calm and ensure your own safety first
  2. Call 112 (National Emergency Number) immediately
  3. Do not move injured persons unless absolutely necessary
  4. Keep the road clear for emergency vehicles
  5. Provide first aid only if trained to do so
  6. Note down witness details and take photos if safe
